Technology Stack

  • LLaMA 3 70B: Quantized & accelerated with vLLM. Provides advanced natural language understanding comparable to GPT-4, with full control over training, tuning, and inference in a private setting.
  • CoreWeave GPUs: Hosted on dual A100s for rapid inference and scalable model execution, ensuring high performance even under demanding legal workloads.
  • ChromaDB: Vector database for efficient document embedding and semantic search, enabling quick retrieval of relevant legal texts across case files and contracts.
  • LlamaIndex: Enables retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines for real-time Q&A, grounding answers in specific firm documents, statutes, and filings.
  • n8n: Automation layer for document ingestion, system alerts, Slack/email integration, and workflow orchestration — no-code and easy to modify.
  • Streamlit: Lightweight, interactive web UI framework used for internal tools. Allows staff to query the model, upload documents, and access analysis results instantly.
  • Security: Enterprise-grade authentication using JWT tokens, IP allowlists, and full audit trails to meet compliance standards and preserve confidentiality.
