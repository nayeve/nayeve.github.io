What’s a Collar Score?

Your Collar Score is a number (0–1000) that reflects how well you’ve mastered positive habits and ditched negative ones. It’s calculated using sensors that monitor your actions and speech, rewarding progress with points and nudging you away from slip-ups. Once you’ve got a score to brag about, share it on social media to catch the eye of potential partners!

Example Posts:

  • "Collar Score: 875! I’m a pro at listening and keeping my cool. #CollarScore #DatingReady"
  • "Level: Chore Master! Trash out, dishes done—ready for a partner who values teamwork. #BetterMe"
